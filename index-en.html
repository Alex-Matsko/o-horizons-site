FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When does a company need an IT infrastructure audit?

Most often when incidents start piling up, 1C slows down or backups have not been tested for years. We document the current state, quantify the risks and prioritise tasks.

How is an audit different from routine administration?

Routine administration is about keeping systems running and putting out fires. An audit is a higher-level look at architecture, backups, security and processes. The outcome is a clear report and roadmap.

What happens after the audit?

After the audit we offer options: a one-off remediation project for critical risks or long-term managed services with a fixed fee and SLA.

How much does the audit cost?

A comprehensive IT infrastructure audit starts from ₽25,000. If you move to managed services, the audit cost is credited to the first month.

How long does the audit take?

Typically 3 to 5 business days, depending on infrastructure size: number of servers, VMs and areas to review. Smaller companies usually take 2 to 3 days.

Do you need physical access to the servers?

No. In most cases remote access is sufficient. We work via VPN or a secure tunnel without an on-site visit. On-site visits are available on request.

What does IT outsourcing include?

It depends on the plan: from monitoring and backup control through to full management of infrastructure, 1C, security and a fractional IT director role.

Can we hand over only part of the tasks?

Yes. We can tailor the scope flexibly: monitoring and backups only, 1C infrastructure only, or specific areas. The final scope is fixed in the contract.
