FAQ

Common questions about IT audit and outsourcing

Short and to the point. Expand the questions you care about instead of scrolling through walls of text.

Most often when incidents start piling up, 1C slows down or backups have not been tested for years. We document the current state, quantify the risks and prioritise tasks. To dive deeper, start with the Start with an Audit section.

Routine administration is about keeping systems running and putting out fires. An audit is a higher-level look at architecture, backups, security and processes. The outcome is a clear report and roadmap: what to fix first and which areas make sense to move to IT outsourcing under our pricing plans.

After the audit we offer options: a one-off remediation project for critical risks or long-term managed services with a fixed fee and SLA. The easiest way to start is via the Contact section — we will discuss your case and suggest a format.
